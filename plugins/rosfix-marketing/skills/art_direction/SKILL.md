## Always resolve these layers

1. order
2. impact
3. identity
4. realism
5. action

## Art-direction rules

- use Swiss alignment and generous negative space
- keep one dominant visual idea
- use realistic technical materials
- control terracotta accents instead of flooding the piece
- use the official logo with intent
- let texture support realism, never replace composition

## Deliverables

Return:

- framing
- lens or crop feel
- lighting
- texture
- typography behavior
- logo placement
- postproduction rules

## Anti-patterns

- gamer neon
- cheap glow
- fake sci-fi overlays
- cluttered multi-panel posters
- generic screwdriver iconography
