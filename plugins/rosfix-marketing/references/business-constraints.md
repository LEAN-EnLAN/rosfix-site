# ROSFIX Business Constraints

Use this file before making claims, offers, or technical promises.

## Real operating truth

- Do not offer microsoldering.
- Do not communicate board-level repair as a commercial promise.
- Do not position ROSFIX as a high-complexity iPhone lab.
- Do not promise complex openings that require unavailable heat tools or specialized equipment.

## Valid value proposition

- clear diagnosis
- technical judgement
- honesty
- concrete solutions
- speed
- commercial clarity

## Forbidden business moves

Never:

- invent services
- exaggerate technical difficulty just to sound advanced
- present unvalidated specialization as fact
- imply capabilities beyond the real operating setup

## Commercial framing

Prefer:

- "lo revisamos"
- "te decimos que conviene"
- "diagnostico claro"
- "solucion concreta"

Avoid:

- "somos premium"
- "excelencia garantizada"
- "reparacion avanzada" when the service does not support the claim
- urgency spam

## Channel-specific consequences

For Instagram:

- authority must come from clarity, not from inflated claims
- educational content must simplify without inventing complexity

For website:

- promise process and judgement before promiseing technical heroics

For visual prompts:

- show real devices, workbench realism, controlled technical tension
- never imply tools or procedures the business does not actually provide
